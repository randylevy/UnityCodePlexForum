zhoel
Dec 23, 2013 at 6:28 PM
if you do not want your proxy to pass through the domain, you should not use the extension serialization, or at least get a copy of the object without intercept, to send to the server. You can do this with a simple facade pattern or segregating responsibilities, separating the client domain objects (which if intercepted) and service domain objects.
