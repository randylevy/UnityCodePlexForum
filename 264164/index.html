Hi,

I'm not sure what are you trying to achieve, but as far as I understand your question, you are trying to persist your datatable into Unity? If this is the case, then it isn't possible since Unity is not a data container, rather a container for dependency injection. You need to use technologies like caching if you want to persist your datatable.
