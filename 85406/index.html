Hi all,

I am trying to gain a better understanding of how to best use Unity with Silverlight.  Also I am new to both DI and Unity so please humor me if the answer(s) seem painfully obvious to you.

First and foremost are there any other Silverlight - Unity examples besides the Stoplight example?
